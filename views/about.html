We believe that the hostel allocation industry in KWASU is broken, and that many students resignedly accept bad services from greedy landlords, caretakers and agents because...well, what can they do? This state of the hostel market forces students to pay more, even as many of these hostel providers offer the most mediocre experience they can get away with. And so, an economy that punishes the students is established. Homely is our redesign of the housing and allocation industry. We're building an efficient world where students get hostels, not just the bare facilities, but comfortable and well structured rooms, with no friction, by our highly trained service providers. No more expensive but poorly structured hostels. We’re here now, and we’ve got you.
